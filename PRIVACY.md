# Privacy Policy

**X-Treeverse** runs entirely in your browser. No data is collected or tracked by X-Treeverse directly when you use or install it.

## Data Collection

- ✅ **No personal data is collected** by the extension
- ✅ **No analytics or tracking** scripts are included
- ✅ **No external servers** are contacted by the extension itself

## Third-Party Services

### X (formerly Twitter)
When you use X-Treeverse, API requests are made directly to X/Twitter's servers:
- These requests use your existing X login session
- Subject to [X's Privacy Policy](https://x.com/privacy)

### Google Chrome Web Store
- Extension installs and updates are handled by Google
- Subject to [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy)

## Permissions

The extension requires these permissions to function:

- `activeTab` - To access the current tab when you click the extension icon
- `scripting` - To inject the visualization viewer into X/Twitter pages
- `storage` - To temporarily store tweet IDs during navigation
- `notifications` - To show error messages if the extension fails to load
